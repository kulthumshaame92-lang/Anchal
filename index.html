Who We Are

A Heartfelt Mission for Every Heartbeat

We sincerely believe that every heart deserves care, attention, and the very best medical knowledge — regardless of geography or circumstance.

Cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of mortality worldwide, yet many heart conditions are preventable with the right knowledge, early detection, and compassionate support. At HeartPulse, we are dedicated to changing that story — one heart at a time.

Our team of cardiologists, cardiac nurses, and public health specialists works tirelessly across multiple continents. From screening programmes in rural Africa to advanced cardiac rehabilitation centres in Europe and North America, we bring world-class heart care to communities who need it most.

We warmly invite you to learn about the conditions we address, the regions we serve, and the real stories of resilience and recovery that inspire our work every single day.

Heart Conditions

Understanding the Heart, Gently

We kindly encourage you to learn about these common cardiovascular conditions. Knowledge is a powerful step toward prevention and healing.

01

Coronary Artery Disease

The most common type of heart disease, caused by plaque build-up in the arteries. With early detection, gentle lifestyle changes, and proper medical care, many patients lead full, active lives. Please don't hesitate to learn more.

02

Arrhythmia & Irregular Heartbeat

When the heart beats too fast, too slow, or irregularly, it can feel quite alarming. We want you to know that many arrhythmias are manageable with medication, lifestyle adjustments, or minimally invasive procedures.

03

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Often called the "silent killer," high blood pressure affects nearly 1.3 billion people globally. Regular monitoring, a balanced diet, and stress management can make a remarkable difference. We are here to guide you kindly through it.

04

Heart Failure

Heart failure doesn't mean the heart has stopped — it means the heart needs a little extra help pumping effectively. With modern treatments and compassionate cardiac rehabilitation, quality of life can improve significantly.

05

Stroke & Cerebrovascular Disease

Closely related to heart health, strokes occur when blood flow to the brain is disrupted. Recognising the warning signs — face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty — and acting quickly can save lives. Every second truly matters.

06

Congenital Heart Defects

Some heart conditions are present from birth. Thanks to remarkable advances in paediatric cardiology, children born with heart defects can now receive life-changing surgeries and treatments. Every little heart deserves the best care.

Global Reach

Heart Health Around the World

We are honoured to serve communities across six continents, adapting our care to each region's unique health challenges, traditions, and needs.

🇺🇸

North America

Heart disease is the leading cause of death in the US and Canada. Our programmes focus on cholesterol management, cardiac rehabilitation, and community screening events to catch problems early.

Cholesterol Cardiac Rehab Screening
🌍

Sub-Saharan Africa

Rheumatic heart disease and untreated hypertension disproportionately affect young people here. Our mobile clinics and training programmes help local health workers detect and treat heart conditions gently and effectively.

Rheumatic Heart Mobile Clinics Youth Health
🌏

South & East Asia

India, China, and neighbouring nations face a growing epidemic of cardiovascular disease. We respectfully partner with local hospitals to provide preventive cardiology, lifestyle counselling, and affordable treatment access.

Prevention Lifestyle Medicine Accessibility
🇪🇺

Europe

European nations lead in cardiac research and advanced surgical techniques. We collaborate with leading institutions across the UK, Germany, and France on cutting-edge heart failure management and valve replacement programmes.

Research Valve Surgery Heart Failure
🌎

Latin America

Chagas disease-related heart conditions and rising rates of hypertension require dedicated attention. Our warm, community-based outreach connects families with cardiac screenings and culturally sensitive health education.

Chagas Disease Hypertension Community Care

Middle East & North Africa

Metabolic syndrome and coronary artery disease are significant concerns. We respectfully consider fasting practices and regional dietary habits while providing evidence-based cardiac wellness programmes.
